What is Big Data?

Big Data refers to the structured and unstructured data that entities of all sizes generate daily. It encompasses everything from social media posts, website clicks, sensor data from smart devices and historical records. This data, when harnessed effectively, can unveil valuable insights and trends that were previously hidden. At its core, Big Data is about more than numbers. It's about knowledge, understanding, and informed decision-making. With the right tools and strategies, Big Data can help businesses, researchers, and individuals.
